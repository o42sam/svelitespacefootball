The academy only accepts applications from male candidates at this time. Other criteria not explicitly stated for determining eligibility will vary with each application as applicants are considered based on information specific to each individual and exceptions can be made as such. Unless otherwise stated, any applications that do not meet the following requirements WILL be denied:
<li>Applicants must have no documented commitment to any other footballing institution. Applicantions by candidates committed institutional education, primary, secondary or tertiary will not be accepted during the duration of said commitments as complete focus on football is required from all members of the academy.
Applicants must have no medical condition(s) that discourage engagement in intense physical activity. This will be corroborated using a valid document showing required medical information.<li>
